Placement

Using both hands, slide the headset down from the top of your head. Place the arms approximately as depicted, being careful to place the sensors with the black rubber insert on the bone just behind each ear lobe.

Correct placement

Notice the 2 front sensors should be approximately at the hairline or about the width of 3 fingers above your eyebrows.


The headset is in position

Press and hold the 2 reference sensors (located just above and behind your ears) for about 5-10 seconds. Good contact of reference sensors is the key for a good signal
